Faculty

Dr. Eun Lee is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Railway System Engineering at Kyungil University, South Korea.

He earned his Ph.D. in Civil and Environmental Engineering from Seoul National University under the supervision of Prof. Seung-Young Ko, with Prof. Dong-Gyu Kim as co-advisor.

His research focuses on transportation planning—particularly decision-making processes, route optimization, and AI-based forecasting—to support social mobility.
