Faculty

Dr. Eun Hak Lee is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Railway System Engineering at Kyungil University, South Korea.

He earned his Ph.D. in Transportation Engineering from Seoul National University (2016.03 – 2021.02).

His research focuses on transportation planning—particularly decision-making processes, route optimization, and AI-based forecasting—to support social mobility.

Contact: e-lee@tti.tamu.edu
